Product Requirements Document (PRD): Integrated Business Platform
(Corporate Website + E-commerce + RFQ System)

1. Project Overview
Goal: To build a comprehensive digital platform that serves as a corporate identity (B2B & B2C), a direct sales channel through an e-commerce store, and a system for receiving and managing Requests for Quotes (RFQ) for custom services or products.

Vision: To provide a seamless user experience that integrates brand storytelling, direct purchasing, and professional quotation requests, all managed through a centralized, dynamic CMS.

2. Target Audience
Individual Customers (B2C): Users looking to purchase ready-made products directly.

Businesses & Institutions (B2B): Clients seeking bulk orders or customized services requiring a formal quotation.

System Administrators: Internal staff (Sales, Marketing, Content) managing the platform.

3. Functional Requirements
A. Dynamic Corporate Website

Homepage: Dynamic hero sliders (images/video), "About Us" section, featured products, and client testimonials.

Dynamic Pages: Ability to create unlimited pages (Privacy Policy, Terms, etc.) via the CMS.

Blog/News Section: Support for categories, tags, and article sharing.

Multilingual Support: Full RTL (Arabic) and LTR (English) support.

Contact System: Dynamic contact forms, Google Maps integration, and social media links.

B. E-commerce Store

Catalog Management: Categorization (Parent/Child), product galleries, technical specs, and variant support (colors, sizes, weights).

Search & Filtering: Live search suggestions and advanced filters (price, rating, brand).

Checkout Flow: Mini-cart, one-page checkout, guest checkout support, and discount coupon integration.

Payments & Shipping: Integration with payment gateways (Mada, Visa, Apple Pay, STC Pay) and shipping carriers (Aramex, SMSA) for automated AWB generation.

C. Request for Quote (RFQ) System

Submission Form: Fields for customer data, company info, request type, and file attachments (PDF/Excel).

"Add to Quote" Feature: Ability for users to browse the store and add specific items to a quote request instead of a direct purchase.

Quote Management: Admin dashboard to track quote statuses (New, Under Review, Sent, Accepted, Rejected).

Conversion: Ability to convert an approved quote into an active order for online payment.

D. Admin Dashboard (CMS)

Content Management: Full control over text, images, menus, and pages.

Order Management: Track sales, update shipping statuses, and manage returns.

CRM & Analytics: Customer database and sales reports (Total sales, top-selling products, RFQ conversion rates).

4. Non-Functional Requirements
Performance: Page load speed < 3 seconds; optimized image delivery.

Security: SSL encryption, PCI compliance for payments, and reCAPTCHA for forms.

SEO: Dynamic Meta titles/descriptions, friendly URLs, and automated XML Sitemaps.
